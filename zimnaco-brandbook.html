01 — Brand Story

Built for the Road.
Engineered for Trust.

Zimnaco Corporation is a tire manufacturing and distribution enterprise grounded in precision engineering, uncompromising quality, and decades of industrial expertise. We serve drivers, fleet operators, and automotive professionals who demand performance and reliability.


Our identity communicates authority, technical competence, and the confidence that comes from building products trusted under the most demanding conditions.

Mission

To deliver world-class tire solutions through advanced engineering, rigorous quality standards, and a distribution network built on dependability.

Vision

To be the most trusted tire corporation in the Middle East and beyond, recognized for performance, safety, and innovation.

Values

Precision. Durability. Integrity. Performance. Safety. These principles define every product we manufacture and every partnership we build.

02 — Logo System

The Mark

The Zimnaco mark is a three-dimensional triangular form enclosing the letter Z. The interlocking planes convey structural integrity, engineering precision, and forward momentum. It is always presented in the corporate navy or approved alternates.

Primary — Light Background
Primary — Dark Background
Monochrome — Silver
Reversed — White

Clear Space & Minimum Size

The clear space around the logo equals the height of the "Z" character within the mark (referred to as 1x). This zone must remain free of any text, graphic elements, or edge boundaries.

Minimum Size

Print: 20mm width. Digital: 32px width. Below these thresholds, use the standalone Z icon without the wordmark.

Wordmark Lockup

When the full "ZIMNACO CORPORATION" text accompanies the mark, it must be set in DM Sans 700, letter-spacing 3px, uppercase. The wordmark sits below the mark with 0.5x spacing.

Logo Misuse

✕ Do not rotate the logo
✕ Do not change logo colors
✕ Do not stretch or distort
✕ Do not add effects or shadows

05 — Photography & Imagery

Visual Direction

Photography should feel industrial, precise, and cinematic. Favor low angles, dramatic lighting, and tight crops that emphasize texture, material, and engineering detail. Avoid stock photography aesthetics.

Mood

Cinematic. Controlled. High-contrast. Images should feel as if they were shot for a premium automotive campaign. Dark backgrounds with selective lighting are preferred.

Subjects

Tires in context: on vehicles, on road surfaces, in testing environments. Close-up tread detail. Manufacturing processes. Road textures. Never isolated product shots on plain white.

Color Treatment

Desaturated with selective color. The navy and chrome palette should influence post-production. Warm highlights, cool shadows. Blue-tinted grading is acceptable.

09 — Brand Voice

How We Speak

The Zimnaco voice is technical, assured, and direct. We speak with the confidence of engineers who know their product. We do not oversell. We state what we do and let performance speak.

Authoritative

We speak from expertise. Every claim is backed by engineering data, testing results, or proven performance. We never hedge unnecessarily.

Technical

We use precise language. Tire specifications, materials science, and performance metrics are communicated clearly without dumbing down.

Direct

Short sentences. Active voice. No filler. The reader should feel they are getting the information they need without waste.

✓ Do Say

"Engineered for wet traction on regional highways. Tested at 120km/h on standing water. Stops 12% shorter than the category average."

✕ Don't Say

"Our amazing tires will revolutionize your driving experience with incredible innovation and unbeatable performance!!!"
